WHAT IS AN UNCONFERENCE?

The sessions will be submitted and chosen by attendees on the day of the unconference. Topics for the sessions can be whatever you want, but we have prepared a list of common mailing-list discussions that can provide starting points.

You don't have to be an expert on the topic to lead a session. If you have a topic you are interested in, try to find a good example and help facilitate discussion!

If a session isn't keeping your attention, don't feel obligated to stay - move to another that needs your voice!

Leave some room for FUN - we want to hear about all kinds of projects, especially the ones you do for fun!
