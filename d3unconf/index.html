d3.unconf

d3.unconf is a one-day gathering of data visualization practitioners. We're meeting to catalyze involvement and knowledge sharing within the d3.js and greater visualization community.

Unconference Style

You make the schedule!

The majority of d3.unconf's agenda will be determined by you, the attendees.

We want you to bring the topics to discuss, whether it's something you've been working on or something you've been playing with.

We'll propose sessions and converge on the final schedule the morning of the unconference. Be prepared to get involved and most importantly, be proactive!





The Purpose of Getting Together

We want to bring the community in one place to accomplish a few things together.

  • Create bl.ocks, fork examples and share ideas.
  • Put a face to a email/twitter/github handle.
  • Have fun!

You Should Attend if You...

  • You are interested in building on your own or other people's examples
  • You have ideas around d3 and data visualization that you're still trying to figure out.
  • You want to meet more people that are trying to apply the latest d3 techniques to visualize data!

Anti-harassment Policy

d3.unconf is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion. We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form. Conference participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the conference without a refund at the discretion of the conference organizers. Please see our full anti-harassment policy

For more information on having an inclusive conference experience see Geek Feminism's Conference Anti-Harassment guidelines.
