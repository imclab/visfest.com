d3.unconf

presented by the Bay Area d3 Meetup

d3.unconf is a one-day gathering of data visualization practitioners. We're meeting to catalyze involvement and knowledge sharing within the d3.js and greater visualization community.

UNCONFERENCE STYLE

The majority of d3.unconf's agenda will be determined by you, the attendees. Have a session you'd like to propose? We'll propose sessions and converge on the final schedule the morning of. Be prepared to get involved and most importantly, be proactive. What do you want to discuss and work on?



GOALS

Some of the goals for this unconference:

  • Lots of forking. forking of gists.
  • Put a face to a email/twitter/github handle.
  • Have fun!

WHO SHOULD ATTEND?

  • You're interested in building on your own or other people's examples
  • You have ideas around d3 and data visualization that you're still trying to figure out.
