WHAT IS AN UNCONFERENCE?

The majority of d3.unconf's agenda will be determined by you, the attendees. Have a session you'd like to propose? Submit it here, and we'll converge on the final schedule the morning of. Be prepared to get involved and most importantly, be proactive. If a session isn't keeping your attention, don't feel obligated to stay - move to another that needs your voice! Unscheduled 'buffer time' between sessions are also great for meeting others.

GOALS

Some of the goals for this unconference:

  • Work towards tangible outcomes and future collaboration
  • Put a face to a name/twitter/github handle.
  • Leave a session inspired enough to blog about it or write some examples (perhaps on bl.ocks)? Other attendees are a perfect first audience!
  • Leave some room for FUN - we want to hear about projects other than your latest Business Intelligence(TM) Dashboard!

WHO SHOULD ATTEND?

  • You should be able to bring examples from your personal or professional work.
  • You don't have to be an expert on the topic to lead a session.
  • Share your "modest moments" - talk about something you're still trying to figure out.
